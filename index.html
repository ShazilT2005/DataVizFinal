Where it Pays to Attend College

By Shazil Taqi

This project explores the correlation between the type of college attended and the average salary of its graduates, aiming to debunk the myth that attending a more prestigious college necessarily leads to higher earnings. Through comprehensive data analysis and interactive visualizations, we reveal insights into the true impact of educational choices on financial outcomes.

Visualization 1: Parallel Coordinates Plot

This visualization shows the distribution of salaries across different majors using a parallel coordinates plot. It helps to compare the starting, mid-career, and growth in salaries among different undergraduate majors.

Visualization 2: Scatterplot of Median Salaries

This scatterplot compares the starting and mid-career median salaries by school type. It visually depicts how different types of schools contribute to the financial outcomes of their graduates.
